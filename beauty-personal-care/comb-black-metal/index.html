3D BAYALAGE COMB - A must have tool for even the most advanced colorists. Only Available Through Seller Oobalie Pro

This Comb has a unique way of micro weaving the hair. It splits any size section into two equal 50/50 subsections. The design has a consistent pattern of comb teeth. Every other gap between comb teeth goes half way down the length of the comb, allowing 50 percent of the section to go all the way down and allowing the split. The closeness of the teeth are intended to create an even micro weave. Creating maximum diffused dimension within every section when pre-lightening or coloring the hair. Working half way down the section length of hair will create a tease like effect due to the closeness of the comb teeth. Allowing the person using the comb to create a seamless dimensional result and blend. The handle including a metal tail, allows the user to maximize the use of the comb. This comb is great for stylists that prefer the metal tail for sectioning and all foil work.
