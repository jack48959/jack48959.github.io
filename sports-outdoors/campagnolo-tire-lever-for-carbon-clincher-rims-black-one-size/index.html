Tire Levers: yes, made of composite plastic Recommended Use: removing tires from carbon clincher rims

The Campagnolo Tire Lever for Carbon Clincher Rims solves a problem that most people don’t realize until it’s too late. Trying to remove tightly-beaded tires from carbon clincher rims with squared-edged or metal tire levers can permanently damage the rim beds or rim beads, compromising the structural integrity of the rim. These strong composite plastic levers are specially shaped to operate similarly to a standard tire lever, but place minimal pressure on any area of the aforementioned rim areas, notably reducing the risk of doing any damage.
