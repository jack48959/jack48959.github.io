Stainless Steel 5" HBT Shank with Sweet Iron Medium Wide Port Comfort Snaffle (MB 05) Copper Inlay Mouth. This mouthpiece is made of polished Sweet Iron which naturally oxidizes. About the mouthpiece: the 05 is one of the mildest Myler mouthpieces in terms of tongue pressure, very similar to the 33. The generously wide port allows room for the tongue to pass under the bit and swallow. However, unlike the solid 33, the 05 collapses slightly although it does not fully rotate on to the tongue like a Level One or Level Two mouthpiece. This gives the mouthpiece more give and may apply some light tongue pressure although it works primarily oﬀ the bars, lips chin and poll. The port on this mouthpiece is only 1 ¼" high, so it will apply little to no palate pressure. Because the 05 oﬀers such a high degree of tongue relief, the horse that goes well in this mouthpiece has a trustworthy and consistent disposition, trained to work oﬀ the leg and seat as well as the hands. This mouthpiece has Independent Side Movement . For a slight increase in tongue pressure, see the 43LP. For similar tongue relief without Independent Side Movement, see the 12. For the same tongue relief and Independent Side Movement, but with no collapse, see the 33. From The Level Best for Your Horse by Dale, Ron and Bob Myler. ©Toklat Originals, Inc.
