EasyCare New Easyboot Glove Back Country Boot The New Easyboot Glove Back Country Boot features a soft, thick comfort cup gaitor to support the soft tissue area. The front shield offers a sleeker fit. This comes standard with a removable rear Snug Strap that assures security in wet or muddy conditions. The modifications allow this boot to be used for both pleasure riding as well as a performance boot. This is an ideal boot for medium to high mileage of 25-50 miles per week. It is also an ideal replacement boot for a lost shoe. Please see the Size Chart and Application Guide. Take measurements following a trim. Correct sizing is imperative for the successful use of these boots. The Easyboot Back Country sizing is not comparable to steel shoe sizes. The Back Country sizing allows for natural hoof growth throughout a six week trim cycle. Measuring the hoof after the horse is freshly shod is recommended if boot is being worn over steel shoes. Can be worn on the right or the left hoof. The Snug Strap can be switched so that both pull rings are on the inside, giving you a left and a right boot. Please allow the horse time to adjust to wearing these boots. Conservative riding is best for the first time your horse is fitted with these. Gradually increase duration and distance to allow your horse to become accustomed. Not allowing time to break in the boots can lead to chafing. Sold individually.
