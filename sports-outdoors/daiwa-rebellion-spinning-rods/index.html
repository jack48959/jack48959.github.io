Daiwa Rebellion Spinning Rods are a new series of premium, tournament-grade bass fishing spinning rods. Daiwa developed the Rebellion series of rods to embody an innovative, mold-breaking spirit that elevates rod performance without also jacking up the product’s price. Designed and crafted with the aesthetics, build specifications and features of custom high-end rods, yet priced just under a $200 MSRP, these rods are one of Daiwa’s new flagship offerings for serious freshwater bass anglers. All models feature Daiwa’s Braiding-X technology to prevent torsion caused by repeated casting and powerful hooksets, which in turn facilitates dramatically improved power, operability, and sensitivity over the life of the rod. Rebellion rods are also equipped with Daiwa’s X45 Technology, which consists of a 45-degree bias construction that prevents twisting of the blank for greater strength, sensitivity, and hook setting power. Additionally, as opposed to standard carbon rods, which have a higher resin content in the space between the carbon fibers, Daiwa’s HVF (High Volume Fiber) rods such as these are made of a carbon fiber compound with less resin. By reducing the amount of resin in the blank, the rods are significantly lighter and more sensitive. Rounding out the impressive list of high end technologies, the Rebellion rods feature Daiwa’s Air Sensor reel seat. This reel seat is built using a carbon fiber material to maximize sensitivity and power, while also lowering overall rod weight. Available in 3 rod length, power, and action variations.
