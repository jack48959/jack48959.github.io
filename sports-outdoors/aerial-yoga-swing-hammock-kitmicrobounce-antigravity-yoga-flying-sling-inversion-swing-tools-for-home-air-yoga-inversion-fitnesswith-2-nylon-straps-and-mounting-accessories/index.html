Precautions for using a hammock:

  1. Do not use too much force during use, so as not to break the contact.
  2. The support point of the hanging hammock must be firm, and the hanging height should not exceed 1M to prevent accidental fall and injury.
  3. Do not tie the bed rope to the support point with sharp objects to avoid damage to the rope.
  4. Avoid carrying buttons or sharp objects that damage the hammock.
  5. Please check the hammock rope before use, the shorter the distance the rope is pulled, the better.
  6. The fabric of this product is not fireproof, please keep it away from fire sources.
  7. Pay attention to the cleanness and flatness of the ground around the hammock, and avoid places where there are gravel, branches and other hard objects.
