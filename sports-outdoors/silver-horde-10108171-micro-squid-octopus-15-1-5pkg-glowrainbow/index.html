The Silver Horde Micro Squid is a type of hoochie often used for targeting smaller species of landlocked salmon, trout and kokanee with trolling applications. For those not familiar with the term, Hoochies are more or less a hollow plastic skirt that can be threaded on a leader, rear hook of a lure or on a jig head. The first hoochies to reach the United States were found on the west coast where anglers employed the 4" - 6" variety for their saltwater Salmon trolling applications. As more anglers began trying them, the overall consensus was these artificial plastic skirts were nearly or equally effective to real bait fish such as anchovies and herring, but simpler, faster and cheaper to use than the real thing.
