The Strip Dredge by Fish Razr is an easy to deploy, and very effective means of calling fish up and into your spread. Fish Razr made this strip dredge to be lightweight, and compact; the strips fold up neatly, and are very easy to rinse off and stow at the end of a long day of offshore fishing. Durable vinyl holographic strips do a great job of emulating a school of bait fish, ripe for the taking. There are three sizes of strip dredge available; small (22″ bar/60 fish), large (32″ bar/72 fish), and super (32″ bar/91 fish). All sizes are sold in a convenient mesh bag. Replacement strips are readily available as well. Strip dredges have become quite popular over the past several years due to their ease of use and relative low cost when compared to more traditional dredges.
