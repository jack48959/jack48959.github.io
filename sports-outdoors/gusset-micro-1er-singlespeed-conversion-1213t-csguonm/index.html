Gusset 1-ER’ Singlespeed Conversion Kit - 16t and 18t 1-Er Convertor Kit Fancy converting your bike to a singlespeed bike? This 1’ER conversion kit will make easy work of converting to a singlespeed and it gives you the option to run a 16t or 18t sprocket. Single Speed adaptor kit for 8/9/10/11 speed Shimano compatible cassette hubs. Pair of CNC machined aluminum spacers offer lateral support to ‘Deep-tooth’ single sprocket. Built-in offset optimises chainline. Supplied with 16 & 18T 3/32’ cogs and lockring. Cog teeth are 3/32’ - 6/7/8 Speed. Not suitable for narrow 5/64’ (9 speed) chains.
