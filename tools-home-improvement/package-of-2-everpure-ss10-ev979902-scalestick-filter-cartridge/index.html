The Everpure EV9799-02 ScaleStick EV9799-02 (SS-10) Filter prevents limescale build-up while providing corrosion protection for water fed equipment including coffee and espresso machines, proofers, combi-ovens, steamers, ice machines, reverse osmosis systems, distillers, misting systems, instant-hot water dispensers. HydroBlend. The EV9799-02 features the Hydroblend compound, which is a unique blend that inhibits mineral scale deposits and provides corrosion protection in low flow water-fed equipment. HydroBlend is unaffected by high temperatures unlike standard scale control additives like polyphosphates. The SS-10’s patented design dispenses a controlled amount of HydroBlend for long-term protection and greater effectiveness of other standard treatments. Operation Recommendations. Pre-existing scale in your equipment is possible which may come loose and clog the equipment. Please remove any loose scale before installing the Everpure ScaleStck EV9799-02 (SS-10). Deliming your equipment prior to use is recommended, but it is not required. It is best practice to inspect the ScaleStick cartridge every 6 months. The SS-10 should be replaced before the HydroBlend is completely dissolved. Note: The Everpure EV9799-02 is a replacement for the AR-10 filter #EV9496-10 (obsolete) for the KleenSteam AR-X. Formerly known as EV9799-01.
