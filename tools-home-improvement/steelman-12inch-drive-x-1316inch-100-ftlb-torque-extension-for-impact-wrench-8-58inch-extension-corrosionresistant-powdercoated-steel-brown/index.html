Protect your wheels, wheel studs, and brake rotors with this STEELMAN torque stick. The STEELMAN 50062 1/2-Inch Drive x 13/16-Inch 100 ft-lb Torque Stick is designed to prevent damage to wheels, studs, and brake rotors by reducing the risk of over-tightening lug nuts which can result in sheared stud bolts, rounded lug nuts, and warped rotors. This stick measures 8-5/8-inches long and has a 13/16-inch socket on the end.
