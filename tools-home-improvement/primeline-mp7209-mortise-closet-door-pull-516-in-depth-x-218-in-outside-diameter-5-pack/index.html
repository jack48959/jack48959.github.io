These stamped steel mortise cup pulls are designed for use on by-passing closet door systems These closet door pulls have a 2-1/8 inch outside diameter and a depth of 5/16 in Satin nickel finish Stamped steel construction Includes small fasteners

These mortise cup closet door pulls are constructed of stamped steel and they come in a satin nickel finish. The outside diameter of these pulls measures 2-1/8 inch and they have a depth of 5/16 inch. These mortise cups rest flush in the door and are used on by-passing closet panel doors to provide a pull for ease of opening and closing, and they are used by many manufacturers. This item includes 5 pulls and 10 nails per pull. Installation tip: to avoid damaging the pulls during installation; use a pair of needle nose pliers to hold the nails while hammering, then finish driving the nails with a nail set to seat them in place.
