TECHNICAL SPECS: Jaw Width 5" (12.7 cm), Jaw Opening 5" (12.7 cm), Throat Depth 3" (7.62 cm), Min Pipe Capacity .375" (0.9525 cm), Max Pipe Capacity 2.5" (6.35 cm). HOW TO USE: Make sure vise is securely mounted to the workbench and the swivel base lock is secure. Open the vise by turning the vise handle counterclockwise. Place item between the serrated jaws. Close the vise by turning the vise handle clockwise until the item is snug in the serrated jaws, continue to tighten until you can no longer move item. Don’t over tighten as this may damage item and/or the vise. When finished working on the item, turn screw handle counterclockwise to release the item.
