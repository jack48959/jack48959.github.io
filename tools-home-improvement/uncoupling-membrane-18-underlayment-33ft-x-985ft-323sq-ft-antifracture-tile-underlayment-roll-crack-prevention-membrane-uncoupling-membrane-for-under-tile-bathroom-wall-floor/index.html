Uncoupling membrane is essential to the process of ceramic tile or stone tile installation as it keeps the tiles from cracks and fractures. It is used to uncouple the tiles from the substrate and keep the stress or movement from reaching the tiles as crack prevention membrane. It is also a great moisture barrier underlayment that keeps water and moisture from seeping through the substrate.
