Since easy solder is at such a low melting point, we recommend using it for sizing and for final seams in soldering projects. The melting point of the medium solder is 1330 Degrees F and the flow point is 1375 Degrees F. Since hard solder is at the highest melting point, we recommend using it for sizing, fabrication and bezel work.
