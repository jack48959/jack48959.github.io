The Cornerstone heavy duty hinges are a reliable and durable solution for any gate around your home or yard. The Cornerstone hinges are heavy duty hinges manufactured from high quality, durable polymer and stainless steel. These hinges are vertically and horizontally adjustable, tension adjustable and self closing. Made for vinyl and wood gates up to 150 pounds, the Cornerstone hinges are heavy duty and will not rust, corrode or sag. Each package contains 2 hinges, enough for 1 gate. These are large hinges- be sure that your gate posts and frames are at least 3.5 inches wide. The Cornerstone hinges are for square gate frames and fit posts 3.5 inches to 6 inches. They will not work on round posts. The gate gap must be between 7/16 inches to 1-5/8 inches. The tension determines how fast or slow the gate will close and can be easily adjusted using the .25 inch allen wrench provided. These hinges are low maintenance and may only require some adjustment or tightening throughout their usage on the gate. All fasteners are included with the hinges as well as detailed installation instructions.
