This comprehensive T track kit is perfect to have on hand in the event you ever need to build any kind of T-track jig or workshop fixture. Kit includes 4 pieces of 24 inch T Track 3/4 inch wide x 3/8 inch thick, 10 each star knobs and 10 each T bolts in various lengths. Universal T-Track has a unique stacked-slot design that allows you to attach any accessory with ¼ inch or 5/16-inch T-bolts or 1/4" Hex-bolts. Kit comes with 4 each T Tracks 24-inch long. 1/4-20 T bolts are designed to slide easily in the T Track slots. Made from Grade 2 steel for durability and then zinc plated to resist corrosion. They can easily be cut to any desired length. Set includes 4 each 1 inch bolts and 2 each of the following lengths: 1-1/2 inch, 2-1/2 inch and 3-1/2 inch. Head size for 1/4-20 bolts are .450 inch wide by .625 inch long and .090 inch thick. Heads are rounded to easily slide along T Track slots.
