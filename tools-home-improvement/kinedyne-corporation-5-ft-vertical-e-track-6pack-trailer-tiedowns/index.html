Kinedyne Corporation - 5 ft Vertical E Track, 6-Pack Trailer Tiedowns
$158.92
Check on amazon

This listing is for 6 pieces of five (5) ft. Vertical E Track. Forged in Alabama, USA from high strength 11 gauge steel. Powder coated in a gray color. Minimum yield 50,000 PSI. APPLICATION: It is designed for use in the trucking industry and is installed vertically on the walls of a trailer. It can also be used on the floor or horizontally on the walls of a motorcycle trailer - the slots are just positioned in a different direction, however there are fewer slots per foot. SPECIFICATIONS: The vertical “E” Track accepts all series E and A fittings. Track is 3.5" wide and raises above the floor .44 of an inch. Slots are 4" apart. Each 5 ft section weighs 6.5 lbs. INSTALLATION: We RECOMMEND installing with 1/4" bolts, lock-nuts, and fender washers (wide diameter). We like to install two bolts (one on each side of the flange) every 12 inches. The “E” track does an excellent job of displacing the weight. Wood screws are OK to use for installation, however we recommend two screws every 6 inches for this application.
