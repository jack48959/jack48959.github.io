8000 grit JIS (1.5 micron) Should be used with water Compatible with Edge Pro and Hapstone Custom made and lapped by Gritomatic Working surface 200 x 83 mm (8" x 3.3")

Suehiro G8 8000 sharpening stones are used for finish sharpening knives on Hapstone and Edge Pro knife sharpeners. G8 8000 stones are made of silicon carbide. All G8 8000 stones are 5-6 mm thick and are mounted on high-quality aluminum blanks. G8 8000 is part of Suehiro traditional Waterstone series which includes Kouga 3000, Rika 5000, Shiramine 6000, W8 8000 (Junpaku) and G8 8000 (Takeiro). G8 8000 is preliminarily lapped and does not require lapping after purchase. G8 8000 should be used with water as a lubricant. Do not soak G8 8000 stones in water. Just splash, or use a water spray. Never store G8 8000 stones in water. Never dry G8 8000 stones near heat. You can use G8 8000 dry but it’s more difficult. The dry usage will help to make mirror edge of high purity without stropping. Check that the stone is in perfect condition and not contaminated. Apply as low pressure as you can to the cutting edge. The stone will be clogged by metal from the blade very fast.
