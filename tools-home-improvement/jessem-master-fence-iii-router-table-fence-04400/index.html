Black Anodized Finish We precision-machined the 6061 aluminum extrusion and finished the parts with a black anodized finish instead of the plastic high boy knobs seen on all other fences. We went one step further and machined the high boy knobs out of 6061 aluminum. Stainless Steel Hardware The entire fence is constructed using stainless steel hardware and other components to give it that beautiful look that will last a lifetime.
