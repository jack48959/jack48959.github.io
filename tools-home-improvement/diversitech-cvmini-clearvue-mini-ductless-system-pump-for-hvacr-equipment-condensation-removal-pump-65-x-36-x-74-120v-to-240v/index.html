Ensure optimal performance of your HVAC/R equipment with the Diversitech ClearVue Mini Ductless System Pump. Designed to automatically evacuate condensate or water discharge, this pump boasts an innovative clear tank that offers visibility into its inner workings, enabling quicker and more accurate troubleshooting. The mini condensation pump senses the rate condensate enters the tank, adjusting its speed and lift accordingly. It also incorporates a floatless sensor, eliminating common issues associated with conventional condensate pumps such as debris fouling, sunken floats, and misalignment. In the event of a clogged line, the built-in overflow switch shuts down the compressor, preventing damage to the equipment or surrounding environment. This pump can be powered with any voltage from 120 to 240V. The included rubber mounting pad allows for easy installation on vertical or horizontal surfaces. The mini condensate pump is constructed from durable aluminum housing and backed by an extended 2-year warranty for added assurance. Measures 6" x 8" x 12.25", perfectly sized for integration with your HVAC/R equipment.
