This white 125VAC 20AMP GFCI(ground fault circuit interrupter) duplex receptacle is a type of circuit breaker which shuts off electric power when it senses an imbalance between the outgoing and incoming current. The main purpose is to protect people from an electric shock caused when some of the current travels through a person’s body due to an electrical fault such as a short circuit, insulation failure, or equipment malfunction. This GFCI outlet meet New Version UL 943&CSA C22.2 No.144.1-16 standard, auto-monitor (self-test) by conducting an internal test to confirm that protected power is available,provides continuous ground fault protection,Testing every 1 minute updates the protection status,Tripping time 0.025 seconds.   3. ON: GFCI has passed self-test and is proving power
  4. OFF: Press‘TEST’button.
