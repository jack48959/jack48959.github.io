Glynn-Johnson 90 series door holders and stops provide long-lasting protection for doors, frames and hardware. 90 Series holders and stops are the most rugged models available for heavy-duty applications. All models incorporate a heavy-duty channel/slide-arm design and offset jamb bracket. The channel is surface-mounted to the door, most often with sex bolts, and the jamb bracket is surface mounted to the jamb, requiring minimal door and frame preparation. This unique design allows for simplified modification of functions, should user requirements change. When the Hold-Open function is not a requirement, Stop-Only models provide a reliable method of door control. Stop-Only models provide the same shock-absorbing capability as Hold-Open models. The Stop-Only model may be used on fire doors. The 90 series Stop-Only models are classified by Underwriters Laboratories (UL) as Miscellaneous Fire Door Accessories. This classification applies to use on either Hollow Metal Fire Doors or Wood Fire Doors. These units may be used on doors of any rating.
