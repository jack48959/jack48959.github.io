Drill America T/A Series Tap America pipe tap is appropriate for threading a wide variety of materials both ferrous and non-ferrous. All pipe taps furnished with a 2-1/2 to 3-1/2 thread chamfer. NPT threads require the use of a “sealer” like Teflon tape or pipe compound.
