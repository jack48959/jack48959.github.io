The Ideal-Tridon Flex-Gear 43 series stainless steel constant torque worm gear clamp has a stainless steel band and housing, a zinc-plated steel screw, and provides constant tension for reduced leakage in changing temperatures. A worm gear mechanism adjusts the clamp’s diameter to precisely apply sealing pressure. A Belleville spring mechanism helps the clamp increase or decrease its diameter as operational or environmental temperatures change. The band’s 9/16” width provides a greater sealing area than clamps of smaller width.
