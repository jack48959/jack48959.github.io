Compact-sized electrical foot switch ideal to turn on and off a device. Includes 8-ft cable with 3-pronged piggyback plug. 15 A, 125 VAC, SPDT (wired normally open). Made in USA. Certified to UL and CSA standards. Mounting bracket for mounting to floor or equipment if desired. Momentary action - Press and hold foot pedal to turn on, release foot to turn off.

The B850-1501 foot switch offers a small footprint in a long-lasting switch that’s perfect for any application requiring a smaller size. The unit features a compact steel housing designed to minimize the footprint, yet still withstand the ruggedness of tough applications, and a mounting bracket to attach the unit firmly to the floor or equipment if needed. Features a 15-amp, 125 VAC-rated switch with an 8-foot cable and a 3-pronged piggyback plug (NEMA 5-15 series): plug the pedal into your North American wall socket, and then plug your device into the back of that plug, and the foot switch will now be in line with the device. This is a momentary foot switch, which means that it turns your product on when your foot is on the pedal, and turns it off when you release your foot from the pedal. Enclosure rating: UL/CSA/NEMA Type 1 and IEC 60529 IP20. For indoor use in dry environments. CSA Certified to US and Canadian standards. Size: 3.0" L x 2.6" W x 1.2" H; 0.5 pounds without cable.
