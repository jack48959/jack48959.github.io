Compact-sized electrical foot switch ideal to turn on and off a device. Includes 8-ft cable with leads on end of cable; rated 15 A, 125 / 250 VAC, SPDT (wired normally open). Momentary action - Press and hold the foot pedal to turn on, release to turn off. Rugged steel case with 2-hole mounting bracket to attach to floor if needed. Made in USA. Certified to UL and CSA standards.

The B850-1502 foot switch offers the small footprint and long-lasting construction of the B-Series, and features a mounting bracket to attach the unit firmly to the floor or equipment if needed, with leads on the end of the cable. Consists of a 15-amp, 125/250 VAC-rated switch with an 8-foot cable and 3 leads to be connected to your device: one ground, one common, and one wired normally open. Ideal for power tools, office and communication equipment, emergency vehicles (fire truck siren or air horn), and more. This is a momentary foot switch, which means that it turns your product on when your foot is on the pedal, and turns it off when you release your foot from the pedal. You will have to hard-wire this to your equipment, so consult an electrical or knowledgeable person for proper wiring advice if needed (or see B850-1501 for the 3-pronged piggyback plug version). Also replaces Pierce B800-7491. Enclosure rating: UL/CSA/NEMA Type 1 and IEC 60529 IP20. For indoor use in dry environments. CSA Certified to US and Canadian standards. Size: 3.0" L x 2.6" W x 1.2" H; 0.5 pounds without cable. Replaces 491-SC36MP. One year warranty on parts and labor. Made in the USA by SSC Controls Company.
