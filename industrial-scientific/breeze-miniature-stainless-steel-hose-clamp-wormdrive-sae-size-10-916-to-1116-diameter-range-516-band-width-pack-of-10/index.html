This Breeze Miniature series all stainless steel worm gear clamp is used to attach fittings to hoses. This Breeze Miniature Series worm gear clamp is suitable for use in air and fuel line applications. Hose clamps attach hoses to fittings to prevent flow leakage. They come in a variety of designs and distribute uniform pressure over the circumference of hoses to bind them to fittings. Hose clamps are suitable for a large number of industries, and commonly used in industrial, electronic, and automotive applications.
