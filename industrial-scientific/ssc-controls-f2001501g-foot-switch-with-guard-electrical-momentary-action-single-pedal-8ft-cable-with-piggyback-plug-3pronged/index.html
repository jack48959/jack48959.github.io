Foot switch with guard and cable. Includes 8-ft cable with 3-pronged piggyback plug for wall outlet. Momentary action - Press and hold foot pedal to turn on, release to turn off. 15 A, 125/250 VAC, SPDT (wired normally open). Made in USA. Certified to CSA and UL standards.

The F200-1501-G is a medium-duty foot switch with a durable die-cast zinc alloy housing, a tough 12-guage steel guard, powder-painted finish, and an 8-foot cable with a 3-pronged piggyback plug. Simply insert the pedal’s plug into your North American wall socket or outlet, and then plug your device into the back of that plug, and the foot switch will now be in line with the device. This is a momentary foot switch, which means that it turns your device on when your foot is on the pedal, and turns it off when you release your foot from the pedal. Features a 15-amp, 125/250 VAC-rated switch. Ideal for power tools, Dremel tools, home and shop tools, meat grinders, industrial, and more. Includes bumper feet and two exterior bolt-down holes for if you want to hard-mount it. Enclosure rating: CSA/NEMA Type 1 and IEC 60529 IP20. For indoor use in dry environments. CSA Certified to US and Canadian standards. Size: 6.0" L x 6.0" W x 5.8” H plus the cable; 5.9 pounds. One year warranty on parts and labor. Made in the USA by SSC Controls Company.
