Innovating Science chemistry kit is used to study diffusion and cell size, and has an activity to create simulated cells (agar blocks) of different sizes to examine how effectively a substance diffuses into a cell over a set period of time. The cells have indicators that help students visualize the degree of diffusion. For hands-on learning, the kit has materials for 15 groups of students, including agar, bromothymol blue concentrate, hydrochloric acid, agar block casting trays, cups, knives, and stirrers. The kit has a teacher’s manual and student study guide copymasters for instructional use, and a safety data sheet (SDS) packet for product information and handling procedures.
