1 Sided Matte Finish - Back side is smooth and glossy 18" x 24" panel 1/4" / 6 mm nominal thickness Laser Safe for Engraving and Cutting Paper Mask Covering on Both Sides

1/4" Black Matte Cast Acrylic with a flat, matte finish on one side and a smooth, glossy reverse side. Each panel of black matte acrylic is protected by a paper mask. This mask helps protect the acrylic during fabrication, reducing the chance of nicks, scratches and laser scorching. After cutting, lasering, etc, simply peel back the masking. Each panel is nominally 1/4" thick and is approximately 0.241 in. As with all cast acrylic, there can be some variations in thickness with each sheet. All panel dimensions are cut to tolerances of +0in/-0.188in. Cast Acrylic is a rigid plastic panel made from PMMA, popularly known by industry names as Plexiglas and Lucite. Acrylic is easy to use with laser cutters and CNC machines. You can also saw cut (miter, circular, jigsaw), as well as sand, polish, and drill holes. It’s a perfect option for household repairs (light covers, replacement window, privacy screens, etc) and for decorative accents. The uses are endless.
