Extend your DIN rail mounting space by utilizing the full depth of your cabinet. 6.0" x 3.5" plate size Clips onto 35mm DIN rail Other styles available RoHS compliant

Save DIN rail space by mounting your devices perpendicular to the DIN rail in your cabinet. This utilizes cabinet depth to fit more devices in the available space. You can mount hubs, routers, and many other devices at a fraction of the horizontal space they would normally consume. This product comes with standard 35mm DIN rail installed on the adapter plate; simply snap your devices on and install the assembly onto your existing DIN rail. The mounting plate is made of sturdy 0.125" thick FR4 material (glass-reinforced epoxy laminate material) and comes with DIN rail installed on the front side of the plate. This product is also available with DIN rail installed on both the front and back sides of the plate (p/n DINP03-6035D-2).
