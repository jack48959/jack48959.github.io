Made from Baltic Birch Plywood Laser Cut Easy to Paint and Decorate Made in the USA

The unfinished Classic Fish Wood Cutout is ready to be decorated, painted or embellished for your next creative project! We make this shape in our production facilities using two thicknesses of quality Baltic Birch plywood that we cut into a variety of sizes with a laser. The Classic Fish Wood Cutout comes in 4 different sizes; Small 4" x 1.75", Medium 8" x 3.5", Large 12" x 5.25", and Jumbo 18" x 8", cut from 1/8" or 1/4" Baltic Birch Plywood. The Small size is sold in quantities of 10; Medium, Large and Jumbo sizes are sold individually. These wood cutouts are designed to be easy to paint and decorate in your next art and craft project. The edges will be brown as a result of the laser cutting process and the top and bottom can have some smoke discoloration on the surface; however, the smoke will not affect paint adhesion and can easily be removed with a light sanding.
