Blend of 100% natural fibers hand-loomed with a silk warp and a cotton weft. Color: Blue, Turquoise, White Content: 50% Silk & 50% Cotton Width: 43 Inches (110 Centimeters) Length: By The Yard

Ikat is a dyeing and weaving technique that has been practiced in traditional cultures in many parts of the world. It is distinguished by the fact that the threads are dyed before the fabric is woven. As a result, the edges of the design’s motifs have a characteristic blurred appearance. Weaving is done by hand and requires skill to maintain the pattern’s register. This piece was hand crafted in the eastern India state of Andhra Pradesh.
