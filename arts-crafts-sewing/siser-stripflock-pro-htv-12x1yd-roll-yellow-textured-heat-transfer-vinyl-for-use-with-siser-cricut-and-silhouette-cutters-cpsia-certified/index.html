Great alternative to embroidery, StripFlock Pro heat transfer vinyl gives logos a raised, textured, suede-like feel. The diffuse opaque colors make StripFlock Pro great for retro designs like those you’d find in a mall in the ’80s, or anytime you want a deep, rich look. StripFlock Pro is thick enough to cover hat seams, but also looks great on bags, demin, and other garments that need textured visual interest. Use StripFlock Pro as a base layer and top it with other materials like EasyWeed Stretch for an embossed look, or layer with more StripFlock! The layering ability of this material with itself and others allows you to say “yes” to multi-color designs. However, please note that for best results, it is recommended that all fluorescent colors be layered using the removal method and the carrier is hot peeled.
