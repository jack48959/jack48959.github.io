WHAT IS A CHIPBOARD? Chipboard sheet is a type of paperboard which is formed using recycled paper that is then pressed and glued together to make a solid, larger structure. The finished product can vary in thickness from very thin to very thick depending on its intended use. Because it’s made from reclaimed materials, chipboard is very affordable without sacrificing quality and more importantly, it’s sustainable which is great for the environment. • • • WHAT ARE THE USES OF CHIPBOARD?
