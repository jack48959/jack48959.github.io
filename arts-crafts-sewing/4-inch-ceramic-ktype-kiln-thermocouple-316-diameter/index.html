K-type Thermocouple, select grade (close grade, class 1), tolerance +/- 1.5 degree C or 0.4% Temperature range: -50 to 2498 °F (Degree Fahrenheit) or -50 to 1370 °C (Degree Celsius) Probe length: 4 1/4 inch or 10.8 cm, Diameter: 3/16 inch or 3 mm Lead wire: 5 ft or 150 cm Miniature K-type thermocouple plug, flat blades, made from thermocouple metal chromel and alumel

This is a high temperature K-type kiln thermocouple for use with ceramic, pottery or glass kilns. It is 4 1/4 inches long and has 4 ceramic insulators, each 1 inch long and 3/16 in diameter. This K-type thermocouple easily fits a 1/4 inch hole in your kiln. The larger 5/16 inch ceramic insulator will seal the hole. It comes with a 5 ft lead wire and a miniature K-type thermocouple connector which will work with most digital thermometers. It is a good alternative to the PY71 from Paragon and works with the kiln thermometer DT2-7 or DT2-9. It will work with Fluke thermometers 53/54 IIB .
