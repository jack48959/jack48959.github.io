Holbein Duo Watersoluble Oil Colors - 40mL tubes - Artist quality pigment in a watersoluble linseed oil with all the handling characteristics of traditional oil paint. A true professional grade artist color that is compatible with both oil color and mediums as well as all water media. The Holbein factory in Japan is a world leader in color technology, manufacturing to exacting quality control standards. Duo Color is a uniform non-sticky, high chroma and highly versatile oil color offering computer controlled permanency and predictable consistency that will produce unique and desired results for the Oil, Acrylic or Watercolor artists.
