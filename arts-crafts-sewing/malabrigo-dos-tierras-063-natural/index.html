Dos Tierras Yarn: Light Worsted DK Weight Yarn, 50% Merino Wool Yarn, 50% Baby Alpaca Yarn, Hand Dyed Needle Size & Gauge: US 5-6 / 3.75-4.0 mm, 20-22 sts = 4 inches Crochet Hook: US I-9 to K-10/12 or 5.5-6.5 mm Wt / Yardage: 100 gm / 210 yards (193m) Texture / 4 ply, Care: Hand Wash, Cold water, Dry flat Made In: Uruguay

Dos Tierras means Two Lands, reflecting the sources of the two amazing fibers found in this ultra-soft yarn. By spinning together 50% of our fine Uruguayan Merino wool yarn with 50% baby alpaca yarn from Peru Malabrigo has created a light worsted DK weight yarn that is as versatile as it is beautiful. When combined the good stitch definition and drape of this four-ply yarn with Malabrigos amazing kettle-dyed semi-solid and variegated colors you have the perfect palette to help show off your stitch work. Baby Alpaca and Merino wool yarn is the perfect DK weight yarn blend!
