SCENTED!! This top coat is loaded with red and white hex glitters in many different sizes. This polish is scented with vanilla peppermint, for a classic sweet mint scent. Softly scented, this polish will not replace your perfume, but even when dry will leave a subtle scent. Layer over pink for a romantic Valentine’s Day polish, or over blue for a rocking mani for the 4th. Please note, the vanilla scent adds slight tan tint to the base that may be noticeable when swatched over white. The tint does lighten as it dries. This polish does look best in one layer, and due to the heavy glitter load, might appear thick. This polish does have larger glitter in it that can be tricky to get out. Shake, or roll polish well before use, pull brush straight up without touching the side, and if needed, put larger glitters on the rim of the bottle to position where you want them. This polish is about 2/3 white glitter and 1/3 red glitter, you may not get large red glitters on every nail. This listing is for a standard full-sized 15ml / .5oz bottle.
