Size 2.25" Height Premium Chenille Shinny Gold Glitters Double Coated Adhesive by 3M

These are sticker that also can be Ironed on. Just peel off the sticker back and attach them to bags, hats, phone case, laptop, book covers or anything else your heart desires! Made of thick chenille and decorated gold glitters with reinforced edge. Iron On Instruction:

  1. Preheat iron to medium setting. (Turn off steam application!)
  2. Peel off sticker back and stick patch onto garment as desired.
  3. Cover the patch with a cloth. (you can use a handkerchief or cotton pillow case)
  4. Press straight down for 30-45 second without moving the iron. (No back and forth!)
  5. Turn the garment inside out and iron press the back of the patch for 20-30 sec.
