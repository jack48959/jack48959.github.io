HOW TO STEM YOUR FLOWERS: (Step 1) - Add a small dab of hot glue to the back of the flower. (Step 2) - Push your stem (wire or bamboo) through the glue, and into the flower. You will want to insert the stem about 1/4 inch into the flower. (Step 3) - Let the glue dry, and check to make sure the flower is secure on the stem. If it seems a bit lose, feel free to add a bit more glue where the flower meets the stem. HOW TO FLUFF YOUR FLOWERS: All you need is just a little bit of water! You can use a bowl of water to dunk the flower in to get it damp. You don’t want your flower to be completely soaked, so a quick dip will do. You can also use a small spray bottle to moisten your flowers. Just spray the petals a few times to get them damp and pliable, you’re ready to fluff!Now that your flower petals are damp, you’re ready to fluff! Just start carefully peeling back the petals to open up the flower. Some of our flowers are designed with a closed flower bud feel. You can easily change this by fluffing. Just by peeling back the petals, you will turn the wood flower from a bud to an open blossom. If you need to, feel free to re-wet your flower by dunking or lightly spraying them with water.
