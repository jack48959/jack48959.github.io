Blue Skull Iron-On Rhinestone Transfer Design by Rhinestop This is a rhinestone heat transfer Iron-on design with a shiny and delicate pink gradient, it can be applied to your favorite shirt or the garment of your preference. Instructions on How to Apply: Preheat iron to cotton setting (about 320-340 degrees). Place the transfer face down on a flat surface and carefully lift the backing from the design. Place the transfer, stone side down, onto the fabric and press into the desired FINAL position with your hand. Place the iron over the design without moving it around and apply steady pressure for 15 seconds. Repeat previous step for other areas of the design, DO NOT overheat as glue will lose its adhesion. If the design has big stones, make sure you keep iron on them long enough to make them stick. Once done ironing, set aside and allow transfer to cool. Slowly and carefully peel the clear plastic sheet away from the fabric. Troubleshooting: If any stones have shifted out of place, carefully put them back into place using tweezers. If stones are not sticking, replace the plastic sheet back onto the shirt, and you may re-iron on the opposite side of the fabric again. Once heat and bonded, there’s no way to reposition design. Care Instructions After application, we recommend to wash garment inside out in cold delicate cycle & hanger dry. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us!
