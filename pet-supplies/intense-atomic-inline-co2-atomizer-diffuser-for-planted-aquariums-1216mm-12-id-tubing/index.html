Nature Aquascapes inline diffuser creates super fine CO2 bubbles less than 0.1mm diameter with highest diffusion rate. CO2 is capable of 100% dissolution in the aquarium water. It also includes high quality suction cups and holder. Designed to be installed outside of the aquarium, inline within the outflow tubing of your canister filter, either below the aquarium or mounted to the aquarium glass. It requires a minimum of 30 psi working pressure. Must use CO2 pressure rated tubing only, not for use with silicone or other brand tubing. Tubing must be acid resistant. Compatible with all tubing sold by Nature Aquascapes. Handle diffuser carefully, do not touch ceramic. Install and remove tubing carefully. Do not use with bubble counter fluid or other bubble counter oils. Fill bubble counter with water only to avoid contamination of diffuser. Never rub or touch ceramic. It is recommended to keep 2 diffusers and rotate between cleanings. If the diffuser and tubing get filled with water, remove from your aquarium and allow it to dry completely before trying again.
