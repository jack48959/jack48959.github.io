Inkjet IRON ON Heat Transfer Paper 8.5" x 11" For Dark fabric - 5 pack (blue line) New INSTRUCTIONS: PRINTING IRON JET INKJET TRANSFER PAPER

  1. Create or select your design on your computer
  2. DO NOT mirror your image or artwork.
  3. Check printer settings and set to single sheet feeding.
  4. Load the paper so that the image will appear on the coated side of the paper.
  5. Print the design and allow it to dry. For best results, trim away unprinted areas of the transfer as close to image as possible. TRANSFERRING- HEAT PRESS APPLICATION
  6. Preheat the garment for a few seconds to remove wrinkles and moisture.
  7. Place the transfer on top of the garment with the image side up.
  8. Cover the entire image area with a Parchment sheet.
  9. Press on the commercial heat press machine for 15 to 30 seconds at 375° F / 175° C using heavy pressure. TRANSFERRING- HOUSE-HOLD IRON ON APPLICATION
  10. Choose a very smooth hard surface at waist level or below, but do not use an ironing board.
  11. Preheat the iron to the highest cotton setting for 3 minutes - do not use warm. Do not use steam (empty all the Water)
  12. Place a pillowcase on the hard surface and iron to remove any wrinkles.
  13. Place the garment on the center of the pillowcase and iron to ensure flatness.
  14. Place the transfer with the image facing side UP in the desired position on the garment.
  15. Using two hands firmly slide the iron vertically up and down.
  16. In 90 seconds, make enough passes to completely iron the shirt two times.
  17. The iron should be halfway off the edge of the transfer when the edges are ironed.
  18. Using two hands firmly slide the iron horizontally side to side.
  19. In 90 seconds, make enough passes to completely iron the shirt two times.
  20. Again, the iron should be halfway off the edge of the transfer when the edges are ironed.
