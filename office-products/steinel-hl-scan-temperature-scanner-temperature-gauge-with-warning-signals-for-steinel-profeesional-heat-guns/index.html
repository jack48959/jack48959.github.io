This temperature gauge is the life insurance for temperature-sensitive materials, e.g. when smoothing leather seats or car wrapping The HL Scan controls the temperature of the workpiece using an infrared beam; Including 9 V Battery Warning when the temperature exceeds or falls below the permitted temperature with an acoustic and optical signal Optical signals: red means “too hot”, green means “ideal temperature” and blue means “too cold”; Tolerance range for the warning can be set individually This temperature measuring device is especially suitable for the Steinel hot air guns HL 1920 E, HL 2020 E, HG 2120 E and HG 2320 E

Full temperature control and never risk damage again. With the HL Scan, the temperature of the workpiece surface is recorded during processing with the hot air blower. As soon as the ideal temperature is either exceeded or undershot, a signal sounds. In addition, the display shows whether the workpiece surface is too hot or too cold. Red means “too hot”, green means “ideal temperature” and blue means “too cold”. The HL Scan can be mounted on the HL 1920 E, 2020 E, HG 2120 E and HG 2320 E hot air blowers. The tolerance range for the warning can be set individually. The optical and acoustic warning can be deactivated if required.
