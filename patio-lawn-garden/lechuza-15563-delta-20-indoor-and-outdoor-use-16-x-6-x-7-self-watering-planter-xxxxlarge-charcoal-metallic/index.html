Discover the perfect way to bring your windowsill to life with the DELTA 20 self-watering windowsill planter. Besides growing your favorite herbs in the kitchen, this versatile planter is also perfect for the bath or the top of the file cabinet in the office. Like most LECHUZA planters, the DELTA 20 comes equipped with a signature water reservoir that supplies water to your plants as needed. Users can easily monitor the water reservoir level with the planter’s water-level indicator, which shows when the reservoir needs to be refilled, taking the guesswork out of plant care and extending time between watering. Also included is LECHUZA’s specially-formulated plant substrate, LECHUZA PON, which separates your potting soil from the water reservoir, aerates the roots, provides your plants with nutrition, and helps regulate the supply of water to your plants. Recommended for indoors use, the DELTA 20 is outfitted with a special liner that can be lifted completely out of the planter so you can easily transport it to wash any dust off your plants or trim the roots.
