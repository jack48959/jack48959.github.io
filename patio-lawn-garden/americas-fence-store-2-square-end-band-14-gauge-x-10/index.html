2" Square end band, also known as brace bands. These items are very versatile and can also be used to attach barb wire and tension wire to end posts. Details How the part fits? The end band couples around the gate post, corner post, and end post to connect top, bottom, or mid-rails, barb arms/barbed wire, tension wire, and more. Where is it installed? The end band fits on the gate, corner, or end post. The band can be placed on the upper, middle, or lower part of the post depending on the use. How many do you need? To attach the top rail, you will need at least (1) end band per termination of fence. For every full stretch of fabric, you will need (2) end bands for your (2) terminal posts. This rule is also true for bottom or mid rail, tension wire, barbed wire, or other use. Specifications 2" OD post size, .29lbs Instructions When using the end band for top rail, end bands should be installed after you install your tensions bands. Your end band will slide over your terminal post adjacent to your rail end. You will then slide your rail end in between the tangs of the end band and place a 5/16” x 1-1/4” carriage bolt (part 11170, not included) through the band and rail end and tighten the nut.
