24 x 6 inch rectangular H-shaped burner for gas fire pit or fire feature 304 Stainless Steel Construction; Female 1/2 inch gas inlet; Drilled Burner Ports Natural Gas Ready; Propane Conversion Available (HBSB24-LP); 90,000 BTU Includes rectangular burner and installation manual Rectangular; 24 x 6 inch length; 2.5 Inch Gas Inlet; 10 inch Flame Height

Build your custom fire pit today using our uniquely designed H-Burner! This H-Burner is specifically designed for use in outdoor fire pits. This burner is 24 in.x 6 in. with a 1/2 in. raised hub design to help shed water. This burner is made of 304 stainless steel 1/2 in. tubing with holes drilled at 30 degrees for inward flame pattern. Each burner port is drilled, not punched, to allow the fuel to properly flow through the burner. This creates a very natural-looking flame pattern and prevents debris from building up, avoiding the annoying whistling sound that is often heard in lesser quality fire rings. Made from stainless steel, this fire ring will not rust, and is suitable for all outdoor environments. This fire ring can be installed by a do-it-yourself installer or by a professional. This ring can be used with either natural Gas or propane (lp) applications however additional components are necessary for propane (lp) applications.
