MageBracket CL Rail-less End Clamp and Mid Clamp Mounting Kits are for solar installation on corrugated metal roof. The mid or end clamp mounting kit can mount solar panel directly on corrugated metal roof without using any rails. It has width adjustable brackets that can adjust to fit most of the corrugated metal roofs. End clamp and self-bonding mid clamp fit any solar panels with frame thickness of 33 mm - 40 mm. If your frame thickness is less than 33 mm or more than 40 mm, we also have corresponding mid and end clamps for such frame. Please send us a message to request such mid and end clamps after purchase. MageBracket mid and end mounting kits are made of aluminum alloy with EPDM rubber gasket seal. Bolt is made of stainless steel. Each kit comes with one MageBracket CL bracket and one mid or end clamp. Mid clamp also includes bonding clip.
