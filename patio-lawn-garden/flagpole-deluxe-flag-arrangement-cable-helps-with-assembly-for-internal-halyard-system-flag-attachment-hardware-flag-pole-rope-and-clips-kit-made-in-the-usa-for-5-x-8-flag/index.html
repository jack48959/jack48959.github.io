Easily attach your flags to the cable assembly with Flagpole Gear’s Flag Arrangement Cable. And when you need to change the size of your flag, no problem. With these clips you can change your flag without changing the entire cable assembly. HOW DO THEY HELP? Flagpole Gear’s flag arrangement cables make attaching a flag to your flagpole easy. But what the arrangement actually does is act as the bridge between your flag and the internal halyard system. It does this as the top of the arrangement clip joins the connecting link of the internal halyard end and the flag. The bottom end attaches to a counterweight and retainer ring that holds the flag’s base straight and close to the pole. You can also use the flag arrangements to add more than one flag to a flagpole as they can link to each other. WHAT ARE THEY MADE OF? Designed to last a long time outdoors, these top-quality flag clips are made of 1/8" stainless steel aircraft cable, aluminum sleeves, and stainless steel thimbles (attached at each end with 2 copper crimps). And they come with 2 quick links. This material ensures they are strong and durable. All arrangements are individually inspected for quality and consistency. WHAT OUR ARRANGEMENTS INCLUDE: Flagpole Gear’s arrangements are supplied with 2 stainless steel quick links for flag sizes 8’ x 12’ and smaller, and 3 stainless steel quick links for flag sizes 10’ x 15’ and larger.
