Halyard rope, or flag pole rope, raises and lowers your flag on most external halyard flagpole systems. This makes them one of the essential components of your flag pole accessories. Order Flagpole Gear’s Halyard Rope in black polypropylene, and you’ll have your flag traveling up and down seamlessly in no time. HOW DO THEY WORK? On an external halyard flagpole system, the rope is placed on the outside of the flagpole. The halyard rope is exposed on the outside of the pole and moves through a pulley mounted at the top. An external cleat allows the rope to be tied off at about 4-5" from the flagpole’s base. This system facilitates easy handling, enabling the flag to be displayed and move up and down as needed.
