10 Feet Wide x 15 Feet Long Flat Sheet Pond Liner in 30-mil Blue PVC. ** THIS LISTING IS FOR A FLAT SHEET POND LINER.** What does that mean? That means that the liner is made from a flexible material that is designed to be draped into your pond, tucking and folding it into position. IMPORTANT - You need to account for your pond’s width, length and depth when choosing the correct liner. Measure your pond by adding the following: Overlap + Depth + Width + Depth + Overlap to find the absolute minimum liner width you require and then repeat the process for the length of your pond. Tip - Be sure to order a liner at least slightly larger than you calculate (you can always retain the extra for future use). Note - All of our liners are made-to-order and most sizes do require us to weld multiple panels together but rest assured, our Quality Team checks every weld before we package the finished product.
